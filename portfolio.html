Clubs

I am a member of Hack Club, and lead my own computing club at my school where I teach the members about coding, code-breaking, and most recently I guided them through their own 'bin' projects!

I lead Astronomy club at my school, where I teach the members (and myself) the Astronomy GCSE specification. We also use telescopes to document the night sky!

Competitions

I competed in the National Cipher Challenge, originally knowing nothing about code-breaking, and cracked different ciphers every week. In the end I won a Silver Medal!

I competed in the Astropi Mission Space Lab competition, leading a team to code a program that can run on a raspberry pi on the ISS to measure its average speed.

I worked in a team to completely dissassemble an old Iphone to analyse its sustainability. We designed a variety of uses for the materials within that were yet to be recycled, and were Highly commended for our entry.
